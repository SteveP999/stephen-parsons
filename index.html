About

Stephen Parsons

Stephen Parsons writes songs about the people and moments that shape a life — family, faith, grief, gratitude, and the love that outlasts everything.

His debut album Touches of the Heart is a collection of deeply personal Christian singer-songwriter recordings, written from the inside of real moments and offered to anyone who's lived through them too.

Released through Hello Texas Records, these are songs built to last — the kind you find yourself humming years later because they said something true.
